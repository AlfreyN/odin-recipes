A Congolese dish made with cannellini beans


Description:

    A congolese dish made with beans and a spicy tomato sauce. This meal is best when served with rice and eaten whilst hot. It takes time to prepare in its entirety however.


Ingredients:
  • 500g cannellini beans
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 3 chopped spring onions
  • 4 garlic
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp of african chilli paste
  • 3 tbsps of tomato puree
  • Coriander
  • Mixed herbs
  • Salt
  • 2 vegetable stock cubes
  • Nutmeg
  • 3 tbsps of olive oil

Steps:

  1. Rinse and soak beans overnight
  2. add garlic powder, onion powder, salt and coriander to a pam
  3. Bring beans to a boil for 30 mins, once tender remove heat and keep water
  4. Add olive oil, onion, grlic and spring onion to a pan until tender
  5. Once tender, add chopped tomatoes, chilli paste and tomato puree and simmer 20 mins
  6. Whilst simmering, add pinch of nutmeg and veggie stock cubes
  7. pour sauce onto beans, add a cup of water and simmer until sauce thickens
  8. Serve with rice
